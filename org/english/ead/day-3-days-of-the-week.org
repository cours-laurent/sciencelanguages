* Days of the week

  | Day | English   | Français |
  |-----+-----------+----------|
  |   1 | Monday    | lundi    |
  |   2 | Tuesday   | mardi    |
  |   3 | Wednesday | mercredi |
  |   4 | Thursday  | jeudi    |
  |   5 | Friday    | vendredi |
  |   6 | Saturday  | samedi   |
  |   7 | Sunday    | dimanche |

* Exercise 1
   1. What day of the week is it today?
   2. Write your routine for each day of the week. For example, Monday
      I do the food shopping for the week. Tuesday I go to the
      gym. And so on.
