3 Phonetics words of the week

3.1 Vowels

ʊ
English Français Phonétique Example
gOOd bon, bonne ɡʊd It's a good day for learning English.
      C'est un bon jour pour apprendre l'anglais.
       
fOOt pied fʊt Look where you put your foot.
      Regarde où tu mets ton pied.
       
pUll tirer pʊl Pull the door if you want to leave the room.
      Tirer la porte si vous voulez quitter la pièce.
       
pUsh pousser pʊʃ You have to push the door if you want to come in.
      Tu dois pousser la porte si tu veux rentrer.
       
cOUld pouvoir (passé/conditionnel) kʊd Could you speak to me in English please?
      Pourriez-vous me parler en anglais s'il vous plaît ?
       
shOUld devoir (conditionnel) ʃʊd You should practice your English everyday.
      Tu devrais pratiquer ton anglais tous les jours.
       
wOUld + verb (indique le conditionnel) wʊd What would you like to drink honey?
      Que voudrais-tu boire chérie ?

On peut remarquer qu'il y a 3 groupes :

  1. Les OO comme gOOd et fOOt (trouvez-en deux autres, attention il y a des pièges)
  2. Les U comme pUll et pUsh (trouvez-en deux autres)
  3. Les OU comme cOUld, shOUld et wOUld (trouvez-en trois autres).

5 Games of the week

5.1 Answers for the mystery word

5.1.1 Level 1

Try to guess in maximum 7 attempts the word that is written with 4 different letters and that is the basis of vocabulary.

Answer: WORD!

5.1.2 Level 2

Try to guess in maximum 7 attempts the word that is written with 7 letters but only 3 different consonant and 1 vowel. Three letters are used twice and only one letter is used once.

Hint: for some people that's the heart of a language.

Answer: GRAMMAR!

5.2 The monkey game

The monkey game is a funny game. Let's suppose you're 4 friends. Friend number 1 says a letter, friend number 2 has to choice:

  1. asking friend number 1 to find a word using the letter in the game
  2. giving a letter and continue the game

if someone can't create a word he becomes a monkey or if someone ask and the friend find a word he becomes the monkey. The monkey can't talk but if someone talk to him then he becomes the monkey and the monkey can play again.

Let's make an example. Peter, Paul, Jack, and Mary are playing the game in that order. So Peter starts with an E, Paul follows with a T, Jack continues with an A, Mary tries an O and Peter asks her to find a word… which word can you find?
