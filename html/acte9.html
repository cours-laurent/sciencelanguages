1 To Be: كان kana

1.1 Conjugation

كوّن koon

I am انااكون ana akounoun
You are (sing. Male) انت تكون anta takounoun
You are (sing. Female) انت تكونين anti takounin
You are (plural M) أنتم تكونان antum takounaan
You are (plural F) أنتن تكونون antun takounoun
You are (2) أنتما antouma
He is هو يكمن houa yakoun
She is هي تكون hiya takounoun
They are (2 M) هما hum
They are (plural F) هُن min
They are (plural M) هم تكونون hum takounoun
We are نحن نكون ahnoun na kounoun
They (dual) هما تكونان huma takounaan

1.2 Non-verbal sentences or nominal sentences gumlatun ismiyyatun جملةاسعية

أنتم معلمون antun mueallimun You (men) all are teachers

أنتن معلمات antun mueallimaat You (women) all are teachers

هما عر بتان huma arabiyaan They (male) are (2) Arabs.

هماعربيتان huma arabiyataan They (female) are (2) Arabs.

هم معلمون hum muellimun They (male) are teachers.

هُن معلمات hum mueallimaat They (female) are teachers.

أنأمن مرسيليا ana min marsilia I am from Marseille

انابخير ana behair I am fine.

اناهناطوعا ana houna tawan I am here voluntarily

انالست مجنو ن ana last majnoon I am not crazy

نحن بشر nahno basher We are humans.

نحن اخنياء nahno aghnya’a We are rich.

نحن المستقبل nahno al mostaqbal We are the future

نحن في الطر يق nahno fe al tareeq We are on the road

أنتما سعيدن. Antouma saeedan You (dual) are happy

I am French = anâ faransiy انافرنسي

I am happy = anâ saîd اناسعيد

I am sorry = anâ tabân انا تعبان

1.3 Verbal sentences = gumlatun fi liyyatun جملة فعلية

We are in the bank = nahnu fi l-bank نحن في البنك

My mother is in school with my brother = ummī fī l-madrasa ma a akhī أمي في المدرسةمع أخي

This chair is very old = hahda l-kursī qadīm jiddan مذ ا لكرسي قديم جدا

1.4 Vocabulary

قلم qalamun Pen

قلمان qalamaane 2 pens

رجل rajul Man

رجلان rajulane 2 men

2 To Have

2.1 Conjugation

I have عذدي ind+e
You have (M) عذدك ind+ka
You have (F) عذدك ind+ki
He has عذده ind+hu
She has عذدها ind+ha
We have عذدنا ind+na
They have (M) عذدهم ind+hum
They have (F) عذدهن ind+hunna
You have (PM) عذدكم ind+kum
You have (PF) عذدكن ind+kunna
You have (2M) عذدكما ind+kuma
They have (2M) عذدهما ind+huma

2.2 Prepositions instead

  • ma a مع :
  • مع الطالب سيارة
  • Ma a t-tālibi sayyāratun
  • The student has a car
  • inda عند :
  • عند الخبيرفكرة
  • Inda l-habiri fikratun
  • The expert has an idea.

عند + ه عنده كتاب جديد inda kitab jadid He has a new book

عندها كتاب جديد indaha kitab jadid She has a new book

عندي كتاب جديد indi kitab jadid I have a new book

Abstract: ل+ي لي ابن و امد li iben wahemd I have a son

لهم ابن و امد lahum iben wahemd They have a son

لنا ابن و امد lana iben wahemd We have a son

Place: في fi هذه الدينة فيها بانان جميلة Hadith almadinat fiya baniya medina This city has beautiful buildings

بيتي فيه اربع غرف Bayti fih arbaa ghuraf My house has 4 rooms

  • ladā لدى :
  • لدىالتاجرمال كثير
  • Ladā t-tāgiri mālun katīrun
  • The merchant has a lot of money
